📖 How XP Works
XP from Sessions
Base rate
1 min = 0.25 XP
Focus rating multiplier applied at session end:
🔥 Locked In
Full XP (1×)
🙂 Decent Focus
Reduced XP (0.7×)
😵 Distracted
Minimal XP (0.5×)
XP from Habits
Easy  Quick win (2–5 min)
5 XP
Medium  Needs effort (5–20 min)
10 XP
Hard  Real effort (20+ min)
20 XP
Sessions
Sessions are manually started and completed. XP is only awarded once you finish and rate the session — interrupted sessions do not count.
A focus rating is required after every completed session.
XP Usage
XP earned from sessions and habits will be used to build and upgrade your island. More to come.
Fair Use
This system works best when used honestly. Log what you actually do — the goal is to reflect real effort, not to game the numbers.
